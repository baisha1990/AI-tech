1) Generative AI - Subset of AI that specializes in content creation capable of creating new content like images/music/text.

2) LLM - Large Language Model - Specific type of Generative AI that specializes in text generation and natural language processing.
Common examples of LLMs include:-
a) ChatGPT from OpenAI
b) Copilot from Microsoft
c) Gemini/Bard from Google
d) Llama from MetaAI
